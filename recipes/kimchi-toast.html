Kimchi Toast

Kimchi Toast is really tasty!

Description

One of my favorite breakfasts to make when I want something fun and crispy in the mornings!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Kimchi
  • Kewpie Mayo
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Steps to make:

  1. Melt a little butter in a saucepan and toss your bread in.
  2. While the bread is toasting, mince up some kimchi.
  3. Flip the bread over, and toss on some of that cheese and also the mayo.
  4. As that side is grilling, use some butter to saute the kimchi.
  5. Toss the sauted kimchi onto the cheesy bread and then place the top slice.
  6. Give the sandwich a flipand then you're good to go!
