Gyudon!

Gyudon is really tasty!

Description

This is a really easy to make and tasty recipe to pull out whenever you wanna make something quickly after a long day at work!

Ingredients

  • Beef Belly Strips
  • Sake
  • Mirin
  • Soy Sauce
  • Rice
  • Onion

Steps to make:

  1. Toss your rice into the rice cooker and cook it.
  2. Chop up your onion lengthwise.
  3. Saute your onion until barely transluscent.
  4. With that, add in the beef belly strips and stiry fry until they're no longer raw
  5. Add in your sake, mirin, and soy sauce.
  6. Serve on top of your rice.
