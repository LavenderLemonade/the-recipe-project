Onigiri

Onigiri is really tasty!

Description

Love making this for breakfast when I need to run!

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Canned TUna
  • Kewpie Mayo
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps to make:

  1. Place your salt and water in little individual bowls.
  2. Wet your hands a bit, then rub salt on them.
  3. Also, before that last step, make sure you combine your mayo and tuna.
  4. With your newly salted hands, grab some rice, and make a little pad.
  5. Make a little depression in it, add the tuna/mayo mix, and then add more rice on top.
  6. Shape into a triangle, and then you're good to go!
